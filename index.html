Slipping Collection (1)

I gaze at
the end of the decayed scent
The curled petals
are silent
The dust under the flashlight
freezes grayly
Quietly mocking at our transiency

Why can’t it stay forever?

Is it that it’s as brittle as
the flame?
Is it that its coldness cannot hold
its destiny?

If everything flow like water
If everything flow like flame
Then why I have to
be contrary to you?
Why there’s always falling
behind love?

The burning is in vain
The memories are in vain
The flushing is in vain
And the ashes are in vain

Maybe only in vanity
I can hold you by
Thump, rustle, patter. Which one is more like
The sound of my heartbeat when I’m with you?
The flapping wings of the butterfly in my stomach became slight
Covered by the bubble burst when you are leaving my view
When wind is the only one that touches my hair, I wonder
At which moment do we have the shortest distance
Is that when our ID cards lay in your pocket together?
Or when we stared at the rising tide as it held our reminiscence?
Sometimes, I think it might be better for me to leave you
Because your charm and selfishness occupy all my mind
So that I would forget myself even when I’m turning blue
And only remember the gurgle when you pour the wine
When I can no longer afford the cost of your kiss goodnight
The only thing I can do is to depict you when I can still write
You exist in the moonlight that looks like ocean
And exist in the moonlight that feels like prison
The lily white scar on my arm comes from you
The butterfly that died in my stomach comes from you

You exist in the moonlight that screams quietly
And exist in the moonlight that illuminates the suffocating fish
The mole that slowly solidifies on my face comes from you
The rain I dreamed of or the rain that actually existed comes from you

You are an eternal epic
or a summer night with an expiration date
You don't belong to me or anyone,
or anything, or any time frame
I can’t keep you or hide from you

But just like I don't belong to the drop
that fall from bird feathers anymore
I decided to save you in tears
and not to write about you anymore
